## Tips for Best Results

### For Learning:
1. **Review wrong answers** - Don't just look at the score
2. **Understand why** - Read the correct answer carefully
3. **Take notes** - Write down topics you struggle with
4. **Practice regularly** - Take quizzes daily
5. **Track progress** - Watch your scores improve

### For Improvement:
1. **First attempt** - Take quiz without preparation (baseline)
2. **Review answers** - Study what you got wrong
3. **Second attempt** - Try again after 1 day
4. **Compare scores** - See your improvement
5. **Repeat** - Keep practicing until 80%+

### For Motivation:
1. **Celebrate improvements** - Even small gains matter
2. **Don't worry about drops** - Everyone has bad days
3. **Focus on trends** - Overall improvement is what counts
4. **Set goals** - Aim to beat your previous score
5. **Be consistent** - Regular practice leads to success

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: When will I see the comparison chart?
**A:** After taking the same quiz at least twice. First attempt has no comparison.

### Q2: How many previous attempts are shown?
**A:** Last 5 attempts for the same question set.

### Q3: Can I hide the correct answers?
**A:** Yes! Click "Hide Answers" button to collapse the section.

### Q4: Does it work in Nepali?
**A:** Yes! All features support both English and Nepali.

### Q5: Will my progress be saved?
**A:** Yes! All results are saved in your browser's localStorage.

### Q6: Can I see answers for old quizzes?
**A:** Currently, answers are shown only for the just-completed quiz. We may add history view in future.

### Q7: What if I clear my browser data?
**A:** Your quiz history will be lost. Results are stored locally, not on a server.

### Q8: Can I print my results?
**A:** Yes! Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P).
